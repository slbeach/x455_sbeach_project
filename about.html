ABOUT ME

Stacey Beach - Visual Designer

I am a graphic designer and artist based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I have 20+ years experience in the arts having recieved my MFA from California College of the Arts and will complete a certificate program in graphic design through UC Berkeley in fall of 2024. I love helping clients bring a project and vision to life through brand identity, logo design, illustration, packaging, or posters for special events.
